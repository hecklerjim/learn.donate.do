About this Project

This project was inspired by this carrd supporting the June 2020 protests for George Floyd. Learn, Donate, Do is meant to educate, inspire and support tangible action. Together we can fight injustice and make the world a better place for all.
Browse our cards to learn about some of the issues we currently face, and to find resources so that you can make your mark and fight for what you believe.
Because our cards offer quick concise links to valuable resources, Learn, Donate, Do is a great medium to disseminate vital information about pressing issues. It is lightweight and sharable.

What this website is not.

Learn, Donate, Do is meant to be used as a jumping off point to begin learning and educating yourself. We are not an intelectual authority on these issues, but rather want to compile a list of useful resources to educate and inspire action.

FAQ

EverythingWrongintheWorld.com is designed to highlight as many of the attorcities in the world as possible. Have you ever noticed how you hear non-stop about a certain issue for a week or two, and then suddenly it completely disapears from the media? We collectively seem to forget that, for example, Hong Kong is still protesting, or that China is still incarcerating Uighur Muslims at an astounding rate. This website serves to remeber all the injustices, not just the 'trending' or #popular ones. And beyond that, it shows how you can directly help by compiling links to organizations working tirelessly to solve these issues.

Browse Attorcities

The purpose of this website is not to act as an intellectual authority on these issues, but rather to compile a list of resources so readers can do their own research. We cannot possibly provide definitive answers to all the problems in the world (although we would like to), but we can provide links to people and organizations who are doing what we deem to be work in the right direction. If you are personally affected by an issue and feel we have misconstrued it, please get in touch so we can rectify our mistake as soon as possible.

This project is only meant to suppliment traditional journalism. It exists as a way to quickly remember various things that are happening in the world right now, and links to learn more about the issues. Learn, Donate, Do works in conjunction with honest investigative journalism. But, unlike most news outlets, we aren't tied to the 24-hour news cycle.
