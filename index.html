What to do?

Sometimes when confronted with this much suffering we choose to ignore, to be apathetic.

So what can we do?

Read, learn and educate.

Hate comes from ignorance.

Demand systemic change.

Demand a system that doesn't require charity.

Donate time and money to causes you believe in.

Yes, a large-scale change is necessary, but grassroots efforts can help us get there.
